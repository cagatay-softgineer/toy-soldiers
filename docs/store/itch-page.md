# itch.io Page Copy — Oyuncak Asker Masa Savaşı (v1.0 #181)

## EN

**Title:** Toy Soldiers Tabletop — Oyuncak Asker Masa Savaşı
**Tagline:** Card-driven toy soldier skirmishes on the living-room table. Physics included. Cat included.

**Short description**
Remember lining up plastic soldiers on the carpet? This is that — grown a rules engine.
Play one order card per turn, watch the little troops rattle under real physics, and pray
the cat stays on the sofa. Hotseat on one PC or a 4-player LAN party with 4-letter room codes.

**Feature bullets**
- 30 order cards across Attack / Defense / Tactic / Event, with keywords (Pierce, AOE, Draw…)
- 4 tower kits with real passives: Machine Gun, Sniper, Shield Bearer, Sapper
- 5 modes: Classic FFA, Quick Duel, 2v2 Teams, Hot Potato King, Sandbox
- 7 rooms with living world events — sandstorms, floods, blackouts, the dog, the cat, ANTS
- Box3D physics: every hit knocks actual toy soldiers around the actual table
- LAN multiplayer that just works: UDP discovery, room codes, reconnect with AI stand-in
- 39 fashion-only cosmetics with a local unlock track; six themed sets
- Fully procedural audio — every blip, meow, and fanfare is synthesized live
- English + Turkish, colorblind-friendly seat colors, reduced-motion mode
- No accounts, no telemetry, no internet required. One exe, one JSON, go.

**Price suggestion:** free / pay-what-you-want (jam-born project)
**Platforms:** Windows 10/11 x64 (portable zip + installer). Linux build unverified.
**Tags:** turn-based, card-game, physics, local-multiplayer, lan, toy, tabletop, low-poly
